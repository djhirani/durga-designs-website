About Durga Designs

A premium South Asian fashion boutique in the heart of Sparkhill, Birmingham — serving families with elegant fashion and expert tailoring.

Who We Are

Birmingham's South Asian Fashion Destination

Durga Designs is a ladies' and kids' South Asian fashion boutique located at Shop 606, Stratford Road, Sparkhill, Birmingham. We specialise in Pakistani, Indian and modest fashion for women and families across Birmingham and the surrounding areas.

Our boutique brings together a wide range of stitched and unstitched suits, modern fashion wear, sarees, abayas, traditional dresses, laces, buttons and kids' collections — all carefully selected to offer quality, variety and elegance.

Through our dedicated tailoring service, Misha Tailoring, we also offer expert stitching, custom fitting and alterations for Pakistani, Indian and Western outfits. Whether you need a brand new suit stitched from scratch or a simple alteration, our tailors bring precision and care to every garment.

Our aim is simple: to bring elegant South Asian fashion, quality finishing and warm, personal service to every customer who visits us.

✂️ Our Tailoring Service

Misha Tailoring
by Durga Designs

Misha Tailoring is the dedicated stitching and alteration service at Durga Designs. Our tailors are experienced in Pakistani, Indian and Western garment construction and alterations. We take time with every garment to ensure a perfect fit and finish.

Whether you're bringing in an unstitched suit you've chosen from our collection, or an outfit from elsewhere that needs altering, we treat every garment with the same care and attention.
