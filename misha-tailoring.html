What We Offer

Our Tailoring Services

From full suit stitching to precise alterations — we bring expert craftsmanship to every garment.

Ladies Suit Stitching

Full stitching of Pakistani and Indian ladies suits including kameez, shalwar and dupatta to your exact measurements.

Dress Alterations

Precise alterations to make any dress fit perfectly — taking in, letting out, hemming and reshaping.

Saree Blouse Stitching

Custom blouse stitching and alterations to complement your saree, ensuring a perfect neckline and fit.

Abaya Alterations

Length adjustments, sleeve alterations, zip repairs and fitting corrections for abayas and modest wear.

Trouser Alterations

Trouser length adjustment, waist taking-in, shalwar stitching and trousers for all styles and fabrics.

Sleeve Adjustments

Sleeve shortening, lengthening and full sleeve restitching for kameez, blouses and all types of tops.

Custom Fitting

Full measurement consultation and custom stitching for outfits that require a perfectly tailored silhouette.

Occasion Wear Fitting

Specialist alterations and fitting for wedding outfits, Eid wear, party dresses and special occasion garments.

Kids Outfit Alterations

Size adjustments, length alterations and fitting corrections for kids' traditional and modern outfits.

Simple Process

How Misha Tailoring Works

From first enquiry to finished garment — a clear and straightforward process every time.

1

Send Your Enquiry

Message us on WhatsApp or call us with your tailoring request. Share your design, fabric or reference photo if you have one. We'll advise on what we can do and any requirements.

2

Visit for Measurement

Visit Durga Designs at Shop 606, Stratford Road, Sparkhill for a measurement consultation. Bring your fabric and any design references. We'll take precise measurements and discuss your style preferences.

3

Confirm Price & Timeline

Once measurements and design are confirmed, we will provide a price and expected completion date. Custom stitching is always priced after consultation — no surprises.

4

Tailoring Completed

Your garment is stitched or altered to your exact specifications with care and attention to detail. We'll notify you when it is ready.

5

Collect or Arrange Delivery

Collect your finished garment from our Sparkhill boutique. Local delivery may be available — please ask when booking. Final payment is made on collection.

Please note: Custom stitching prices are confirmed after measurement consultation and may vary based on fabric type, design complexity and style requirements. A deposit may be required for custom orders. All tailoring is bespoke to your measurements — please ensure you can visit the shop for fitting.
